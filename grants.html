Invest in Downtown Griffin: DDA Grant Programs

Welcome to the City of Griffin Downtown Development Authority (DDA) grant gateway.

Available Grants

To accelerate economic revitalization, drive high-impact private capital investment, and preserve our historic visual architecture, the DDA administers three competitive, discretionary, performance-based incentive programs tailored to different tiers of development:

  • Downtown Catalyst Flex Grant Program

    High-tier gap-financing for major redevelopment projects.

    Engineered as a high-tier gap-financing mechanism for major commercial, industrial, or mixed-use redevelopments with a minimum capital threshold of $500,000. This performance-based grant bridges final funding gaps, scaling up to 10% of verified project costs.

  • Facade Improvement Grant Program

    Quarterly matching reimbursement for visible exterior improvements.

    A responsive, quarterly matching reimbursement tool offering up to $5,000 (on a strict 50/50 cost match) to restore, stabilize, and enhance permanent architectural exteriors visible from public rights-of-way.

  • General Business Assistance Grant Program

    Support for maintenance, permitted work, and localized commercial growth.

    Designed to support core building maintenance, allowable permitted work, and localized commercial growth within the district footprint and offering up to $5,000 (on a strict 50/50 cost match).
