Heck Chapel

Historic chapel located in downtown Griffin, GA. Heck Chapel was purchased by the City of Griffin Downtown Development Authority for the purpose of blight elimination and economic development. The property is one of several Broad Street properties that will be included in a public RFQ process in the near future.
